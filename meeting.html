Steps to Join

  1. Enter the Meeting ID and Passcode provided by the organizer
  2. Enter your display name as you want to appear in the meeting
  3. Click "Join Microsoft Teams Meeting"
  4. Choose to join via browser or the Teams app (if installed)
  5. Allow camera/microphone access if prompted
  6. You'll enter the meeting lobby until the organizer admits you
