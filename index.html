Welcome to ZubLab - Where Learning is an Adventure!

ZubLab is a language center for children, offering in-person classes in three languages: Arabic, English, and Rohingya. For a simple monthly fee of 1000 BDT, kids can learn and practice in a supportive, face-to-face environment!

ZubLab invites you to join us and develop your child's language skills.

For Kids

Interactive lessons, educational games, and creative activities designed specifically for young learners ages 4-12. Learn while having fun!

For Parents

Track your child's progress, access learning resources, and get expert tips on supporting your child's educational journey.

Educational

ZubLab aligns three key languages: Arabic, English, and Rohingya. Its focus on Arabic enables learners to read the Quran accurately through the foundational Qaida method, while English and Rohingya support broader communication and understanding. Developed by ZubLab's Committee.
