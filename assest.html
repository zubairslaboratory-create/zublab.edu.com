Frequently Asked Questions

Is Zublab compliant with children's privacy laws?

Yes, Zublab is fully compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) in the United States and GDPR-K (General Data Protection Regulation for children) in the European Union. We never collect personal information from children under 13 without verified parental consent.

What type of data does Zublab collect from my child?

We collect minimal data necessary for educational purposes only:

  • Progress and learning analytics (to personalize education)
  • Username and avatar (no real names required)
  • Parent email (for account creation and notifications)
  • No location data, contact lists, or photos are collected
How can I review or delete my child's data?

Parents have full control over their child's data:

  • Access the Parent Dashboard to view all collected data
  • Request data export or deletion at any time
  • Contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@zublab.com
  • We process deletion requests within 72 hours
Does Zublab share data with third parties?

We never sell or rent children's data to third parties. Limited data sharing occurs only with:

  • Educational partners (anonymous, aggregated learning data only)
  • Service providers under strict confidentiality agreements
  • When required by law or to protect safety

All third parties are vetted for compliance with privacy standards.

What security measures protect my child's data?

We implement multiple layers of security:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure servers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Anonymization of data where possible
  • Two-factor authentication for parent accounts
